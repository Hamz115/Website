Plant Controller - SCADA

SCADA (supervisory control and data acquisition) is a category of software applications for controlling industrial processes, which is the gathering of data in real-time from remote locations in order to control equipment and conditions. Our team of engineers is qualified and able to build, implement and troubleshoot any problems with SCADA Systems. We provide the following services for SCADA Systems:

  • Location survey and data acquisition
  • Building of SCADA system from scratch
  • Training and troubleshooting

Repair Don't Waste!

PCBs are central to all Electronic products, which include PLCs, HMIs, Motor Drives, and the like! The cost of repairing a circuit board is about 10% of buying a new one, so why are we throwing them away so easily? We at Automated Control Systems can help you save money and the environment by repairing your damaged products! With PCB repair, you can extend the life of your products and make them more cost-effective. We offer several services, including:

  • PCB repair and rework for damaged boards
  • Replacement of components like connectors, capacitors, resistors, etc.
  • Training and troubleshooting
